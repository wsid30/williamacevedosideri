Will's Main Page

Yakuza 0

A crime story told through the eyes of two men: Kazuma Kiryu and Goro Majima. A solemn story of betrayal, land grabs, and melodramatic Yakuza intrigue. These somewhat heavy themes are surrounded by wonderfully strange characters and side quests involving buying land, racing stock cars, and hitting baseballs at the batting cages.

Final Fantasy XIV: Shadowbringers

An online entry into the long running RPG series, Shadowbringers is the newest expansion to the online game. Following the story of the Scions Seventh Dawn to the First, our heroes must save the multiverse from The Eigth Umbral Calamity. While I haven't really played a major MMO since Wizard 101. FFXIV has a somewhat complicated combat system taught through a slow drip of progression that makes long strings of abilities feel like a second nature. Although the game has an initial buy in and a monthly fee, it is well worth the price.

Resident Evil 2: Remake

A reimagined take on the classic game, RE2 remake marks my second time playing through a Resident Evil game. Through the use of familiar and new rooms in the Raccoon City police department, Claire and Leon's journey is full of scares, science fiction, and action.
